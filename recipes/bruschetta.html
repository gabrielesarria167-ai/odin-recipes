Tomato Bruschetta


Description

Bright bursts of color and its juicy flavor put tomato bruschetta top of mind when families want a simple, good Italian appetizer.
Freshness stands out every time vibrant, red tomatoes and green basil come together: that nice look makes it great for sharing at all kinds of gatherings, from backyard cookouts to casual weeknight dinners.
Busy families like how a quick bruschetta recipe fits busy days: it works great as a start to a family dinner or serves as a reliable dish at parties where everyone grabs a bite.
No surprise why home cooks lean on easy tomato bruschetta for gatherings big or small: consistent good results, super family-friendly, and always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of homemade bread
  • 500 g of tomatoes
  • 8 leaves of basil
  • Oregano to taste
  • Olive oil to taste
  • Fine salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. First, wash the tomatoes, divide them in half and then cut them into cubes.
    Pour the cut tomatoes into a bowl and add the basil leaves, previously washed and dried.

  2. Season with a pinch of oregano, salt, pepper and oil.
    Stir well and let the flavors blend for about 30 minutes.

  3. Meanwhile, cut the bread into slices.
    After the resting time, heat a grill and place the slides of bread on it.
    Grill them on both sides until toasted.

  4. Top the bread slices with your tomato salad, and drizzle a bit more oil.
    Let rest for a couple of minutes, then serve the bruschettas!
